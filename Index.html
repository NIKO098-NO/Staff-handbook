MCRP Staff Handbook

Last Updated: 2.6.2026
Version: 1.0

1.0.1 – Introduction

Welcome to the Miami City Roleplay (MCRP) Staff Team. As a staff member, you are entrusted with maintaining a safe, fair, and immersive environment for all players. Staff members shape the culture, standards, and integrity of the community. This handbook guides staff roles, expectations, procedures, tools, and disciplinary protocols.

1.0.2 – Purpose

Clearly define staff responsibilities and authority. Establish professional procedures. Provide guidance for conflict resolution. Maintain transparency and accountability.

1.0.3 – Scope

Applies to Founding Management, Moderation, Administration, Senior Staff, and Specialized Roles. All staff must follow these policies.

1.0.4 – Staff Expectations

Professionalism, consistency, responsiveness, confidentiality, teamwork, and integrity are required at all times.

1.0.5 – Staff Structure

Moderators, Administrators, Internal Affairs, Management, Directors Board, Ownership Board, Foundation Board, Server Holders. Moderators enforce rules, Admins oversee moderation, Senior Staff manage complex incidents, Chief Staff hold final authority.

1.0.6 – Staff Activity and Accountability

Moderators: 5 hours/week and 3 actions logged. Administrators: 7 hours/week and 5 actions logged. Inactivity triggers review.

1.0.7 – Staff Training Requirements

Melonly proficiency, disciplinary procedures, conflict resolution, and evidence collection training are required.

1.0.8 – Confidentiality and Ethics

All investigations remain confidential. Misuse of authority or leaks may lead to dismissal.

1.0.9 – Reporting and Documentation Standards

User, Action, Reason, Staff Name, Date, and Proof must be logged for every disciplinary action.

2.0.0 – Moderation Tools and Procedures

Use Melonly for tickets, logs, punishments, and escalation workflows.

2.0.1 – Discipline Guidelines

Escalation thresholds include warnings, kicks, mutes, and timeouts before bans.

2.0.2 – Common Rule Violations

RDM, VDM, FRP, NITRP, NLR, GTA Driving, Staff Disrespect. Punishments must remain consistent.

2.0.3 – Roleplay Guidelines

Staff cannot roleplay while on duty and must stay neutral during moderation.

2.0.4 – Chain of Command

Follow hierarchy for disputes. Chief Administrators make final decisions.

2.0.5 – Ticket Management

Handle tickets professionally and escalate when needed.

2.0.6 – Escalation Procedures

Minor handled by Moderators, Moderate by Admins, Severe by Chief Admins.

2.0.7 – Communication Standards

Professional language only. Reports must be clear and accurate.

2.0.8 – Evidence Collection

Video, screenshots, or logs required for serious disciplinary actions.

2.0.9 — Outside Server Activity Policy

No active staffing in competing servers without approval. Progressive discipline applies.

3.0.1 – Event Moderation

Ensure fairness and document incidents during events.

3.0.2 – Staff Development and Evaluation

Periodic reviews, feedback sessions, and continued training required.

3.0.3 – Staff Standards

Maintain professionalism, fairness, and a positive experience for members.

3.0.4 – Activity Requirements

Minimum 35 messages per week required to remain active staff.

3.0.5 – Ticket Rules

Only the claiming staff member may handle the ticket. Manual channels required if system fails.

3.0.6 — DO NOT ASK FOR RANKS

Do not request promotions. Ranks are earned through activity and performance.

3.0.7 — Progressive Discipline System

3 Warnings = Infraction, 3 Infractions = Strike, 2 Strikes = Demotion, 3rd Strike = Termination.

3.0.8 - Staff Formats

M, A, IA, MGT, E, COS, SS, SM, D, DD, AD, O, CO, ACO, F, CF, ACF, SH, SHA.
